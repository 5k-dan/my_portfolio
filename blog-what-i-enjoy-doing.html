What I Enjoy Doing

Playing the Clarinet and Piano: My Musical Journey

Music has been a significant part of my life. I’ve been playing the clarinet for six years, starting in high school and continuing through college. Whether it's performing at church services or adding a special touch to weddings, playing the clarinet allows me to express myself and bring joy to others. It’s more than just an instrument to me—it’s a way to connect with people and create beautiful moments.

I also love playing the piano. For three years during high school, I played for my church, and it was one of the most rewarding experiences of my life. The piano has always been my go-to instrument for relaxation and creativity. Whether I’m improvising melodies or learning new songs, it’s a hobby that brings me peace and fulfillment.

Gaming: A Weekend Adventure with Friends

When I’m not playing music, I enjoy diving into the world of video games. My favorite games include Fortnite, Roblox, and Rocket League. Gaming is not just about competing—it’s about teaming up with friends, strategizing, and having fun. Weekends are my favorite time to jump into these virtual worlds, where I get to relax and enjoy friendly competition.

Each game has its unique thrill—whether it's building structures and surviving in Fortnite, exploring user-created worlds in Roblox, or scoring an epic goal in Rocket League. Gaming is my way of unwinding and staying connected with my friends, no matter where we are.

Cooking: From Nigerian Delicacies to American Favorites

Cooking is another passion of mine, and I love experimenting with different cuisines. My favorite meals to prepare are traditional Nigerian dishes like jollof rice, fufu, and pounded yam. These recipes not only remind me of home but also allow me to share a piece of my culture with others.

On the other hand, I enjoy cooking American dishes as well. Preparing meals like salmon with rice or fried potatoes is my way of blending flavors and trying new things. Cooking gives me the chance to be creative in the kitchen while enjoying the satisfaction of a well-prepared meal. Whether I’m making something for myself or for others, the joy of cooking never fades.
